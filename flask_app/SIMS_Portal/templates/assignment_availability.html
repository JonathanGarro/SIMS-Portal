How Availability Reporting Works

The date ranges you see on this form change depending on the day of the week when you fill it out. If you are filling this form out early in the week (Monday through Thursday), then you will only see the remaining dates for the current week, ending on Sunday. If you are filling this out later in the week (Friday through Sunday), then you will see the remaining dates for this current week and all of next week.

When you check a day, that means you are committing to volunteering to support on tasks that match your technical profile.
