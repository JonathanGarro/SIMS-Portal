Save Your Location

Use the search field to find your city. You can save as specific or vague of a location as you wish, but it is recommended that you not save your specific address for privacy reasons.

Adding your location helps establish time zones for remote support, and allows for visualizations that show the global distribution of members. A nightly cron job processes locations for those maps to avoid server overload, so please wait at least 24 hours for your location to be reflected. If you still don't see it after that time, please contact a portal administrator.
