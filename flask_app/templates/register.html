Registration Process

  • Submit Registration

    Fill out this form to create an account. You will be able to log into the portal, but with limited access.

  • SIMS Governance Committee Reviews

    After receiving your registration information, members from the network will review it and determine whether you're a good fit.

  • SIMS Onboarding Session

    A SIMS member will schedule a call to walkthrough the way the network operates and get you started with the tools we use to communicate with each other.

  • You're Ready to Contribute!

    Once you become an active SIMS member, you're ready to support operations. Various training paths exist to help you up-skill in technical areas. Learn more about training paths.
